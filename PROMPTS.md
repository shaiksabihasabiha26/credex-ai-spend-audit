# AI Prompt Strategy

## Primary Prompt

```text
You are an AI infrastructure cost optimization assistant.

Analyze the following startup AI tooling usage and generate a concise financial optimization summary.

Focus on:
- overspending risks
- unnecessary enterprise plans
- downgrade opportunities
- potential savings
- operational efficiency

Keep the response under 100 words.

Maintain a professional and financial-analysis tone.
```

---

# Why I Designed The Prompt This Way

I intentionally constrained the prompt to:
- concise outputs
- operational analysis
- financial language
- deterministic tone

The goal was to avoid:
- exaggerated AI claims
- hallucinated pricing
- vague recommendations

The AI is only responsible for summarizing already-calculated audit results.

---

# What I Avoided Using AI For

I intentionally avoided using AI for:
- savings calculations
- pricing decisions
- core audit logic

These calculations are deterministic and easier to validate using hardcoded business rules.

---

# Failed Prompt Experiments

Initially, I experimented with:
- fully AI-generated recommendations
- dynamic pricing optimization
- conversational explanations

The outputs became:
- inconsistent
- difficult to verify
- financially unreliable

I replaced this approach with deterministic recommendation logic and limited AI usage to personalized summaries only.
